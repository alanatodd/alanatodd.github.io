Wonderfell Characters:

Wonderfell is a Alice in Wonderland reimagined in the urban fantasy genre. Alice falls through an NYC manhole cover discovering surreal underground worlds.

Character Lineup: Alice, Mad Hatter, Queen of Hearts

The Mad Hatter and the Queen of Hearts character designs are inspired by Black Dandyism. Alice, who comes from the topside world is inspired by modern alternative fashion.

Alice Character Sheet

Alice is a a young girl whose family just moved back to NYC. Her parents take to the city like ducks to water, even after so many years gone. Still unsure of the life here, young Alice is struggling to navigate through the hustle and bustle of NYC.

Mad Hatter Character Sheet

This Mad Hatter is inspired by Black Dandyism. Black Dandysim is a cultural movement started by the enslaved Black people in the United States to show resistence and assert individuality when they were thought of as a commodity. Traditional Black dandies have worn various suits, but modern Black Dandyism explores more nuanced ways of self-expression using fashion. The point is to use dressing well as a tool to show your individuality, and challenege sterotypes and societal limitations. This Mad Hatter is just as weird, eccentric, and crazy as the original, just with a different flair.
