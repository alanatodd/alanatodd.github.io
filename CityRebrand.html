Ocho Rios Rebrand:

This is a project where I was tasked to rebrand a city. I chose Ocho Rios, Jamaica or Lil Ochi as locals call it.

Lil Ochi Heroes Bonfire Event:

I created an event hosted in Lil Ochi. This is a made-up event inspired by Jamaica's 7 Heroes. The goal is to honor them while also getting the youth more involved with their history and culture. The event would last for 7 days and each day would be dedicated to a specific hero. It would be a celebration of culture and freedom.

Olympic Pictograms:

What if Lil Ochi hosted the Olympics? These are pictograms depicting Olympic sports, some tradtional and others new.
